ABOUT ALBA DENIM

THOSE THAT KNOW

Alba Denim is a luxury denim brand based capital city Edinburgh in Scotland. New on the luxury denim market, our Edinburgh workwear manufacturing facility dates back to 1913 when we initially converted unusable sails into trousers and dungarees for Scotland’s tradesmen.

The Products

THOSE THAT KNOW VINTAGE

All of our denim is weaved on original 1950s looms, ring spun and at a relatively heavy weight of 12 ounces, people have a love for all things authentic, meaning that "old things" become popular once again.

THOSE THAT KNOW FASHION

It’s said that: What goes around comes around – and that saying is never truer than when applied to fashion. The humble shuttle loom produced many other types of fabric since it was invented, Alba denim being just one of them.

THOSE THAT KNOW QUALITY

A cinch back and a red selvage all combine to produce a high quality, luxurious pair of raw denim jeans. Alba denim are not pre-shrunk and come in one style although they can be customised to the wearer’s specification. Each retail outlet will have specialist washing machines as part of the display so the customer can have thier jeans finished to their own preferences.
